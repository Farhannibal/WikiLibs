SNORD78 (also known as U78 ) belongs to the C/D family of snoRNAs . It is predicted to guide 2'O-ribose methylation of the large 28S rRNA subunit at position G4593. [1] The snoRNAs U44 , U47 , U74 , U75 , U76 , U77 , U79 , U80 and U81 , also of the C/D family, share the same host gene with U78 (protein non-coding). [2]
